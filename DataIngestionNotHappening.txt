HuntingBookmark
| where TimeGenerated > ago(30d)
| summarize Entries = count(), Size = sum(_BilledSize), last_log = datetime_diff("second",now(), max(TimeGenerated)), estimate  = sumif(_BilledSize, _IsBillable==true)  by _IsBillable
| where last_log > 228769
| project last_log
